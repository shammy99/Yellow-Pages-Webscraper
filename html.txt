      <h2>
       Unsure about calling the tree stump removalist for that decaying tree?
      </h2>
      <p>
       We’ve put together some ways to tell if your tree is diseased and requires a tree surgeon or tree removal services:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        If the highest branches are no longer expanding or growing it may indicate internal rotting and root loss.
       </li>
       <li>
        Leaves that are turning yellow with brown tips suggest the tree root system is too deep and are preventing the tree from growing.
       </li>
       <li>
        Moss or ferns growing on the tree as a result of insects and bacteria eating into the healthy bark.
       </li>
       <li>
        Hit the tree with a rubber mallet to see if it is hollow and has therefore been eroded from the inside.
       </li>
       <li>
        Mushrooms growing at the base.
       </li>
       <li>
        Hanging and dead branches.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Considering these warning signs, have a visual inspection of your tree to determine if it's time to call a...
